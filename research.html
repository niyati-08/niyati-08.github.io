Research Interests

Our laboratory focuses on understanding the structure, dynamics, and functional mechanisms of biologically important RNA molecules using solution-state NMR spectroscopy and complementary biophysical and molecular biology approaches.

Major Research Areas

  • RNA Structural Biology
    Structural and dynamic characterization of structured RNAs, long non-coding RNAs, and regulatory RNA motifs.

  • RNA–Protein Interactions
    Understanding molecular recognition between RNA and RNA-binding proteins in viral and cellular systems.

  • MALAT1 Triple Helix
    Investigating the structure, stability, ligand interactions, and functional significance of the MALAT1 3′ triple helix.

  • RNA G-Quadruplexes
    Biophysical and structural analysis of RNA G4 structures and their interactions with proteins and small molecules.

  • Solution-State NMR Spectroscopy
    Application of multidimensional NMR spectroscopy to study RNA structure, dynamics, and molecular recognition.

  • RNA-Ligand Interactions
    Characterization of RNA-small molecule interactions using NMR, ITC, MST, EMSA, and related approaches.

Experimental Approaches

  • Solution-state NMR spectroscopy
  • RNA in vitro transcription and purification
  • UV thermal melting analysis
  • Circular dichroism spectroscopy
  • ITC, MST and BLI binding studies
  • EMSA and fluorescence-based assays
  • RNA mutational and functional studies

Current Directions

Current projects in the lab focus on RNA structural dynamics, RNA-protein recognition, non-coding RNA biology, RNA structural motifs in disease pathways, and environmental modulation of RNA structure and function.

Selected Publications (2019 onwards)

  • Structural and mechanistic studies on RNA–protein interactions in viral and cellular systems.
  • Biophysical characterization of long non-coding RNA structures and RNA regulatory motifs.
  • Studies on m6A-dependent RNA recognition mechanisms.
  • Investigations of RNA G-quadruplex structures and interactions.
  • NMR-based analysis of structured RNAs and RNA dynamics.
