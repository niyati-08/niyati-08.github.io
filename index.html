Welcome

Welcome to Dr. Niyati Jain’s research group. Our lab focuses on RNA structural biology, solution-state NMR spectroscopy, RNA–protein interactions, RNA-ligand recognition, and biologically important non-coding RNA structures.

Research Focus

  • MALAT1 3′ triple helix structure and function
  • RNA G-quadruplexes and RNA regulatory motifs
  • RNA–protein interactions in viral and cellular systems
  • Long non-coding RNAs and gene regulation
  • Solution-state NMR and biophysical characterization of RNA

Current Projects

Our current work explores how structured RNA elements respond to proteins, small molecules, environmental stressors, and cellular regulatory factors. We combine NMR spectroscopy, biophysical assays, molecular biology, and collaborative computational approaches.

Techniques

  • Solution-state NMR spectroscopy
  • ITC, MST, BLI and EMSA
  • RNA in vitro transcription and purification
  • RNA structural probing and stability assays
  • Reporter assays and RNA functional validation
